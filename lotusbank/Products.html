Sale-Based Financing Solutions

Murabaha (Cost-Plus Financing)


Through our Murabaha financing, the Bank purchases assets on your behalf and sells
them to you at a disclosed profit margin. This solution is ideal for asset purchases,
allowing you to manage large expenses with transparent payment terms.


Istisna' (Manufacturing Financing)

Our Istisna’ financing supports projects that involve manufacturing or construction. We
finance the production of custom goods and deliver them as per your needs, making it
an excellent solution for construction projects and large-scale manufacturing orders.

Bai Muajjal (Deferred Payment Sale)

With Bai Muajjal, the Bank can provide goods or assets with a deferred payment
option. You receive the asset immediately, with the flexibility to pay at a later date.
This solution is especially helpful for businesses and individuals managing cash flow.

Lease-Based Financing Solutions

Our lease-based solutions offer flexible financing options for various asset and service needs, with all arrangements compliant with Islamic leasing
principles.

Ijarah for Service (Service Leasing)


Our Ijarah for Service product provides a unique, Shariah-compliant way to finance
essential services such as education, medical treatment, professional fees, and other
personal or business-related services. Under this arrangement, the Bank covers the
cost of the required service, allowing you to benefit from the service immediately. In
return, you pay a fixed rental fee over an agreed period, with no interest involved.



Ijarah (Asset Leasing)

With our Ijarah leasing product, the Bank enables you to access the assets you need—
whether it’s property, vehicles, or equipment—through a Shariah-compliant leasing
arrangement. Under Ijarah, the Bank purchases the asset on your behalf and leases it
to you for an agreed rental payment over a fixed period. This structure allows you to
utilize the asset without any interest payments, as Ijarah is structured around ethical
leasing, not traditional financing. Throughout the lease term, the Bank remains the
owner of the asset, assuming the risks of ownership, while you benefit from its use. At
the end of the lease period, you may have the option to assume full ownership of the
asset through a final transfer agreement. This makes our Ijarah product a flexible, fair,
and Shariah-compliant solution tailored to your needs, without compromising Islamic
principles.

Equity/Partnership-Based Solutions

Our partnership-based options are structured to foster collaboration and shared growth between you and the Bank.

Musharakah (Joint Partnership)


With Musharakah, the Bank partners with you by investing in a project or business,
sharing profits according to a pre-agreed ratio. This partnership-based solution
supports ventures where both parties contribute resources and expertise, offering you
the benefit of shared risk and reward.


Mudarabah (Profit-Sharing Investment)

The Bank supports customers through Mudarabah savings/investment accounts,
where profits from Shariah-compliant investments are shared between the Bank and
depositors, ensuring a fair return. This promotes Shariah-compliant business growth.

Fee/Service-Based Solutions

We understand the value of specialized services, and our fee-based products provide flexibility and support tailored to your needs.

Wakalah (Agency Services)


Through our Wakalah arrangement, the Bank can act as your agent for various tasks,
such as managing investments or handling financial transactions. You benefit from our
expertise, while we earn a service fee for efficient management, ensuring transparency
and clarity in our dealings.



Kafalah (Guarantee Services)

As part of our guarantee offerings, we provide Kafalah, a service where the Bank acts
as a guarantor to a third party on your behalf. This service is ideal for situations
requiring assurance, such as securing contracts or credit facilities, ensuring your
interests are safeguarded.
